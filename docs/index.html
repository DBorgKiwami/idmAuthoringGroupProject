Immediate Care When Every Second Counts

Our Emergency Department provides 24/7 critical support for life-threatening conditions and acute injuries. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please dial 999 or 112 immediately or proceed to our emergency entrance.

The EHIC allows anyone insured by a statutory health insurance scheme in an EU member state, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, or Switzerland to access medically necessary, state-provided healthcare during a temporary stay.

Key Features:
  • Coverage: It provides treatment under the same conditions and at the same cost (free in some countries) as people insured in that country.
  • Limitations: It is not a substitute for travel insurance. It does not cover private healthcare or costs such as mountain rescue or being flown back to your home country.
  • Validity: It only covers state-funded providers and is intended for short-term stays (like tourism or exchange programs).
